# Ghost Hunter Extension                                                        
  
A browser extension designed to help job seekers identify and track ghost job postings. It will analyse job posting dates, assess the likelihood of receiving a response, and provide a company rating using company data from online sources like Glassdoor, LinkedIn, and Indeed. Additional features include Salary Benchmarking, Job Post Source Credibility, and Job Post Legitimacy Reports to further assist users in making informed decisions.

## Features

- Ghost Job Detection: Analyzes job posting dates and employer response patterns to flag potential ghost jobs.

- Response Probability Assessment: Estimates the likelihood of receiving a reply based on company history and user feedback.

- Company Ratings & Reviews: Fetches and consolidates company reviews and ratings from trusted online sources.

- Salary Benchmarking: Provides salary insights based on industry standards and company pay trends.

- Job Post Source Credibility: Evaluates job posting sources to determine their legitimacy and credibility.

- Legitimacy Reports: Generates detailed reports to help users make informed decisions about job applications.

- Bookmarking & Tracking: Allows users to save and track job postings, marking interactions and updates.

- User Feedback System: Collects real-time feedback from job seekers to improve job post accuracy and relevance.

- Custom Alerts & Notifications: Notifies users about job postings that meet specific criteria or appear suspicious.
